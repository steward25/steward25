## 🚀 About Me

🎯 Kotlin-first Android Developer with 9+ years of experience building high-performance 
native apps — from consumer phones to set-top boxes powering living rooms — using Kotlin, 
Java, C++, and Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP).

📱 Skilled at crafting pixel-perfect, responsive mobile and tablet UIs with Jetpack Compose, 
Coroutines, and modern Jetpack libraries, with a strong focus on performance and UX consistency 
across screen sizes.

📺 Specialized in Android TV/STB development — one of Android's most niche and high-demand 
domains. From custom launchers and D-pad navigation to XDS compliance and STB certification, 
I've shipped production TV experiences end-to-end.

🌐 Backend-savvy enough to own full features independently:
* Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Messaging, Remote Config, Crashlytics)
* XMPP for real-time messaging
* VoIP & WebRTC for call and video features
* Spring Boot (Kotlin & Java) for RESTful APIs and backend services

🧱 Passionate about modular architecture, clean code, and scalable KMP solutions — and equally 
committed to mentoring junior developers and leaving every codebase better than I found it.

👨‍🏫 Strong communicator, team collaborator, and mentor to junior developers.
